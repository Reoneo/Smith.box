About smith.box Naming Service

Welcome to the smith.box Naming Service, your gateway to a fully tokenized identity on Web2 and Web3. With smith.box you can register a unique NFT domain in seconds. Each domain you mint is an on-chain NFT, giving you complete ownership and portability.

Key Features

  • Web3 name registration as an on-chain NFT
  • EVM-compatible domains for broad ecosystem support
  • Smart XRP PayString integration for seamless payments
  • Family-friendly subdomain tools for shared identities
  • Developer APIs and SDKs for custom integrations

How It Works

Simply connect your wallet, choose a name, and mint your NFT domain. The process takes less than a minute and your new digital ID resolves across dApps, wallets, and payment rails instantly.

Pricing

Our transparent pricing starts at 0.01 ETH per domain, with optional add-ons for premium names and extended registration terms. No hidden fees.
