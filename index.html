What is a Digital ID?

A Digital ID is a unique identifier that can be used to securely manage your online presence.

About Our Service

Welcome to Smith.box, your gateway to a revolutionary Web3 service that empowers users to create unique digital identities through personalized subdomains. With our platform, you can generate subdomains like jake.smith.box or jessica.smith.box, offering a secure and easily recognizable digital ID for all your online activities.

At Smith.box, we leverage blockchain technology to ensure each Digital ID is tokenized and securely stored, guaranteeing full ownership and control over your identity. This prevents unauthorized access or tampering, ensuring your digital presence is always protected.

Your Digital ID also functions as an email address, such as jake@smith.box, merging your online presence with seamless and secure communication tools. This integration makes managing your digital identity more efficient and secure.

Our Digital IDs can be used for various purposes, including secure logins on websites and applications, personalized email addresses, and even wallet addresses for cryptocurrency transactions. The versatility of our service ensures you can use your Digital ID across multiple platforms without compromising security or privacy.

Smith.box is dedicated to decentralization and user empowerment. Our platform uses blockchain technology to create a secure, immutable record of your digital identity, ensuring it cannot be altered or duplicated without your permission.

The blockchain securely stores your digital identity in an immutable ledger, guaranteeing that your Digital ID remains unaltered and preventing unauthorized modifications. This ensures that your identity is always safe and under your control.

Our tokenized IDs are easily transferable, allowing you to retain control over your identity even if you switch platforms or services. This feature ensures that you always have ownership and flexibility over your digital presence, no matter where you go.

Built on the robust infrastructure of ENS (Ethereum Name Service), 3DNS, and the Optimism blockchain, our service integrates with ICANN and Intercap registry. This provides a decentralized, reliable foundation for your digital identity, ensuring its security and reliability.

Smith.box offers a comprehensive Web3 solution for managing your digital identity, providing unmatched security, convenience, and versatility. Whether you need a secure login, a unique email address, or a reliable wallet address, our Digital IDs meet all your needs.

Based in the United Kingdom, Smith.box is committed to pioneering the future of digital identities. By utilizing advanced blockchain technology and integrating with key internet services, we provide an innovative, dependable, and future-proof service.
